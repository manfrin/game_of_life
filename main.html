Conways Game of Life

Conway's Game of Life is a simple set of rules for making cell automata. Each tic, a cell lives if it has 2 or 3 neighoring living cells, it dies otherwise ('starvation' or 'overcrowding'). A cell is born if it has exactly 3 neighbors and is not currently living.

This page will seed a new board every pageload. If things stop or are uninteresting, just hit refresh.
